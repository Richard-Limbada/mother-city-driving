LEARNERS
CLASSES

Learner Classes costs R250 per class
in which we revise all the questions and

DRIVING LESSONS

Driving Lessons prices start at a rate of R160
per hour and a package of 10 lessons costs R1500.
We offer a Pick up and drop off service.
The Lesson hour begins when the Learner gets into the car and not when the learner starts driving.

TEST DAY
CAR HIRE

Car hire including a lesson on the day of the driver test costs R550 if the test is in Southern Suburbs of Cape Town but car hire price could increase depending on how far the testing station is.
